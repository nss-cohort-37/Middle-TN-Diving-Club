GENERAL INFORMATION
  • Open to boys and girls age 10 and up. (Must be comfortable in deep water)
  • The Middle TN Diving Program is available for beginner lessons through competitive team on both 1 meter (low board) and 3 meter (high board)
  • AAU & USA Diving competition is also available, with competitions against other regional and national competitors.
  • High school & middle school programs representing all local schools.
  • Bronze Group: beginners and younger athletes.
  • Silver Group: divers with some experience (All 5 diving groups represented).
  • Gold Group: divers with all competitive groups, high school competition ready (11 dive list).
TIMES & FACILITIES
  • Practice times are scheduled throughout the week.
  • Practices are held at Glencliff HS (managed by the YMCA of Middle TN) and/or Tennessee State Univ - Gentry Complex Pool
  • TSU Pool has one 1 meter and one 3 meter boards Glencliff Pool has two 1 meter boards.
  • Tuesday & Thursday 6-8pm at Glencliff HS
  • Monday & Wednesday (hopefully starting in October) at TSU Pool
  • First ½ hour is dryland training & 1 ½ hrs on the diving boards.
  • Other times may also be arranged. (Ex. Meets, Fridays & Weekends)
REQUIRED INFORMATION
  • Must be a member of AAU Diving the amateur athletic association, or USA Diving
    1. This covers insurance. (Required by facilities)
    2. Also allows competition against other local - regional programs and teams.
  • Must complete and return information form & waiver (signed & dated by parent)
